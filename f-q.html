Why did you choose the name "The Moms Co."?

When trying to think of a name for our company, we wanted a name which reflects our core values and our mission. A mission to help moms make safe, natural, toxin-free product choices for themselves and their family. The Moms Co. became an obvious choice because we wanted you as a mom to be at the heart and soul of everything we do and every decision we make here. We aim to be the benchmark for:

1.Honesty. We offer what we promise. No hiding in the fine print. We accept no leeways of any kind when it comes to you and your babies.

2. Keep It Simple. It’s quite tempting to talk in non-human scientific jargons and cheesy brandlines. Yet we refrain. You deserve full transparency so you can make the best decision for yourself and your baby.

3. Experts Advice. We unwaveringly work with only the best in class doctors, researchers and experts in their respective field. No compromises.

4. Safety Comes First. We referenced the highest global safety standards as our starting point. Our experts have since then continually helped us raise the bar further by considering leading scientific evidence to improvise our products & processes. We are certified 100% Toxin-Free and Made Safe by Safe Cosmetic Australia's Toxic-Free Campaign.

5. Details Matter, A LOT. Everything we do - we have a snarky obsession to detail whether it be formulation, product, packaging or design. For when you deserve the best, why settle for less?

What do you mean by Nature In. Toxins Out.?

It’s our unwavering product philosophy - we only use the most effective natural ingredients and have zero tolerance to any potentially harmful chemicals. Our 6-step No Compromise Safety Process helps ensure our natural products meet the highest global standards for safety and have only the best, natural ingredients in them.

JOINING THE FAMILY

What is The Moms Co. family?

The Moms Co. is a company built for moms. Over the years, we hope to expand our community of moms who don’t compromise to together create the safest, best range of products for ourselves and our families. Products we can use, confidently and fearlessly.

We welcome all moms who come in touch with The Moms Co. to join our mission, and join our family. 

THE MOMS CO. PRODUCTS

Are your products safe for breastfeeding?

Yes, all our products are safe for breastfeeding moms. However, we suggest you avoid the nipple area where the baby feeds.

Do you use parabens, sulphates, mineral oil, SLS/SLES, PEGs or any other harmful chemicals or toxins?

No. All our products are made to the highest global safety standards and we test each ingredient against the leading toxic-free databases globally to ensure that our products are free from all potentially harmful chemicals. We check each ingredient against:

  1. Environmental Working Group (EWG) Skin Deep

  2. Whole Foods Premium Skincare database

  3. Paula’s Choice Ingredient Directory

  4. Safe Cosmetics USA

  5. Safe Cosmetics Australia

What is the shelf life of your products?

All products go through vigorous testing under varying weather conditions, from 0 degrees to 60 degrees. This ensures that our products can be safely used for upto 2 years from their manufacturing date. They are best when consumed within 6 months of opening.

Are your products really safe and effective? How are your products different?

Yes. We are stubborn about using only the best natural ingredients. We have spent countless hours with experts looking into every ingredient for its effectiveness and safety. Even if there was an iota of doubt or possible side-effect studied about any ingredient  across the world, we made sure we do not use it. We are setting a new bar in the industry because of our attention to even the minutest of details.

That being said, we are constantly trying to outdo our products and processes. If you have any feedback on our product, we want to hear from you.

Are your products tested on animals?

No, and they never will be. However our products have been tested at the top research labs for optimal safety.

What is pH and what are they for The Moms Co. products?

Skin pH is the natural level of acidity or alkalinity of your skin. For best results, The Moms Co. products are pH balanced and created to leave your skin feeling soft, supply and moisturized.

What brands do you carry?

The Moms Co. exclusively designs and distributes all our own products – maintaining intense focus on quality, product innovations, health & safety, and affordability.

Are these products exclusive to The Moms Co.?

Yes, the products have been created exclusively by experts for The Moms Co.

Do I really need the Trial Kit?

The Trial Kit is an easy way for you to experience our products.

Are you using any synthetic fragrances in your products?

No. We use essential oils as fragrances for our products. Compared to other regular products out there, this means that over time, our product fragrances might smell a bit different. This is fine (and a sign of purity too).

Could any of your products trigger allergies?

All our products are dermatologically tested for safety and irritation. However, not everyone has similar skin. We recommend you do a patch test on your arm to see if the product causes any irritation before use.

What is My Account?

After you become a member of The Moms Co. family, we create your very own, personalized Account page. Your account lets you access and update your user information, connect your social media channels as well as track your order and past purchases.

How do I sign up with Facebook?

Click Sign Up on the top of the page and when prompted choose Sign up via Facebook. Authorize The Moms Co to allow access to your basic profile and email. The Moms Co. will never post on your behalf without your permission. Once you confirm your information, your account will be created.

Why am I asked to confirm my name and email after I login to Facebook?

If you are new to The Moms Co, we want to ensure that we have your preferred email on our database to send you all the communication including emails related to your purchases, newsletters, new offers and discounts. You can update your email preferences at anytime in your My Account settings.

If you have an existing account at The Moms Co., please sign in with your account email address and password and we will connect your accounts. If you connect your accounts, you will be able to login either via Facebook or email going forward.

What do I do if I log in/sign up from Facebook and can’t access my past purchases?

If you do not see your past purchases please contact our customer care at +91-124-33138120, Monday - Friday 10AM - 6PM (IST) and our team would be happy to sort it for you. We do not ask for your password over the phone.

How do I update my shipping address and preferences?

After you join, we will create a personalized Account page which you can access by logging into your account at The Moms Co. Here, you will be able to update all the personal information saved at our end. You can also call our Customer Care  and our executives would be able to do it for you.

How do I unsubscribe from The Moms & Co marketing newsletters?

We always want to be connected with you and make sure you are the first one to hear about our promotions or discounts whatsoever. However we can understand that it becomes too much to catch up on your end sometimes thus you can anytime go into your account preferences and unsubscribe or you could also press unsubscribe on a newsletter of ours which would immediately redirect you to your newsletter settings.
